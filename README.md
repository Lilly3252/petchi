# Petchi 🐾

**Petchi** is a Discord bot inspired by Tamagotchi, allowing users to adopt, care for, and interact with virtual pets directly in Discord. Keep your pet happy, fed, and healthy while having fun with friends!

---

## Features

- 🐶 **Adopt Pets** – Choose from a variety of cute animals to start your journey.
- 🍎 **Feed & Care** – Feed, play, and take care of your pet’s needs.
- 🎮 **Mini Games** – Play games to earn coins and rewards for your pet.
- 📈 **Growth & Levels** – Watch your pet grow and evolve over time.
- 🌐 **Multi-Server Support** – Your pets are tied to you, available across servers.

---

## Commands

<!-- COMMANDS_TABLE -->

| Command  | Description                        |
| -------- | ---------------------------------- |
| `adopt`  | Adopt a new pet                    |
| `battle` | Battle with another pet            |
| `buy`    | Buy an item from the shop          |
| `care`   | Take care of your pet              |
| `daily`  | Get 50 coins! (available each 24h) |
| `feed`   | Feed your pet                      |
| `play`   | Play with your pet                 |
| `status` | Check the status of your pet       |
| `train`  | Train your pet                     |
